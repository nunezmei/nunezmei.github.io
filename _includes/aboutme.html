How do you do?

My name is Mary Ann Denman. I am a UX designer based in Seattle, Washington.

I have always had a servants heart. It makes me happy to be able to do something for someone that they can’t do for themselves. Taking care of family when they’re sick, helping friends in need, being protective of my siblings, whatever it was. I like to be there and provide for the people that I care about. Nursing allowed me a career where I actually got personal enjoyment from what I did. My family and friends even joke about what a people pleaser I am and that I can be self - sacrificial at times. Almost always true but it’s what I loved to do until UX came.

Why shift to UX? Why not? Combining UX Design and my nursing background into a career is a great mix of elevating my desire to help people even more. My previous experience as a healthcare professional is of an advantage in creating usable and accessible solutions that are absolutely necessary to making user-centered experiences.
